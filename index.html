Michigrid

Michigan Energy Independence for the AI Age

A proposal to build large-scale energy infrastructure that powers future industries while returning economic benefits to Michigan residents.

The Vision

Artificial intelligence, advanced manufacturing, and electrified infrastructure require enormous energy capacity. The regions that produce abundant power will attract the next generation of industry.

Michigrid proposes building a modern statewide energy system designed to power these industries while ensuring Michigan residents benefit directly from the infrastructure built in their communities.

By investing in renewable generation, grid-scale storage, and advanced transmission systems, Michigan could become a net exporter of energy while supporting AI development and future manufacturing.

Community Benefit

Michigrid envisions community energy cooperatives and public-benefit structures that ensure energy development creates long-term value for Michigan residents rather than extracting wealth from the state.

Starting in Muskegon

A pilot project in the Muskegon region could demonstrate how modern energy infrastructure creates jobs, attracts investment, revitalizes communities, and builds the foundation for statewide expansion.
